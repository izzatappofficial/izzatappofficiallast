What's a chatbot?

  • Feb 15, 2021
  • Our Solution

A chatbot is a computer program that is designed to simulate human-like conversation. Everything a chatbot knows is “taught” to it by a human.

You can think of it as software that stores a bunch of templated responses. It picks and chooses its responses based on how it has been taught by it’s human programmers/designers to make decisions.
